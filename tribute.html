Elon Musk

South African entrepreneur Elon Musk is known for founding Tesla Motors and SpaceX, which launched a landmark commercial spacecraft in 2012.

Here's Elon Musk's timeline:

  • June 28, 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • June 1989: Moved to Canada to attend Queen's University
  • 1992: Went to University of Pennsylvania to Study Business and Physics
  • 1995: Started Zip2, a Web Software Company
  • February 1999: Sold Zip2 to Compaq for US$307 million
  • March 1999: Co-founded X.com, a financial service and payment company
  • 2000: X.com merged with Confinity to form PayPal
  • October 2000: Musk got Fired from PayPal
  • May 2002: Founded SpaceX, a space exploration company
  • October 2002: eBay acquired PayPal
  • July 2003: Tesla, Inc was incorporated
  • February 2004: Musk joined Tesla
  • 2006:Co-founded SolarCity and SpaceX Success
  • 2007: Musk became the CEO of Tesla
  • December 2015: Announced creation on OpenAi, Artificial Intelligence company
  • September 2016: Revealed Mars colonization exploration architecture details
  • January 2019: Traveled to China for the groundbreaking of Tesla's Shanghai Gigafactory
